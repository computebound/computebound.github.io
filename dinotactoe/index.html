Dino Tac Toe

A game designed to make kids feel good.

Builds confidence, not frustration. For iPhone & iPad.

Why Dino Tac Toe?

Most games are either too easy (boring) or too hard (frustrating). Dino Tac Toe adapts to your child - challenging enough to be engaging, but never so hard they feel discouraged.

Win a few games? Bronty gets smarter. Struggling? Bronty eases up. Your child stays in the sweet spot of learning and confidence.

Features

  • Smart Difficulty - Levels up after wins, eases down after losses
  • 2 Player Mode - Play with a friend or family member
  • Kid-Safe - No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection
  • Made for Kids - Perfect for ages 4-9
  • Beautiful Design - Fun dinosaur theme kids love
