Dino Tac Toe - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2025

The short version: Dino Tac Toe does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

Dino Tac Toe does not collect any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account creation or sign-in
  • Access your camera, microphone, or contacts

Local Data Storage

Game settings (such as AI difficulty preferences) are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Dino Tac Toe does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Dino Tac Toe is designed to be safe for children of all ages. We do not collect any data from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
